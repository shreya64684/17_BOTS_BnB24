Book Your Appointment Easily with SMS!

You can now schedule appointments directly from your phone through SMS messaging.

Here's how it works:

  1. Open your messaging app and send a new SMS message to **[Your Twilio phone number]**.
  2. In the message body, type the following:
    • Keyword: Start your message with the keyword **[Your booking keyword]** (e.g., BOOK).
    • Date and Time: After the keyword, add the desired date and time of your appointment in **YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM format** (e.g., 2024-02-23 15:00).
  3. Example message: BOOK 2024-02-23 15:00
  4. Send the message. You will receive a confirmation SMS once your appointment is booked.

Important Notes:

  • Please use the exact format for date and time.
  • Currently, you can only book one appointment at a time via SMS.
  • For any questions or assistance, please call us at [Phone number] or visit our website.

We are still under development, but we are excited to offer this convenient way to book appointments!
